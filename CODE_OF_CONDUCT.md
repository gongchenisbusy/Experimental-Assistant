# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors and maintainers must communicate respectfully, evaluate ideas on evidence, welcome good-faith questions, and protect the privacy and safety of research users.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, deliberate disclosure of private information, falsification of scientific evidence, and attempts to obtain or publish credentials or restricted research data.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `ea_feedback@163.com`. The maintainer may edit or remove content, reject contributions, restrict participation, or refer security matters to the process in `SECURITY.md`.

Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical. Retaliation against a reporter or reviewer is not permitted.
