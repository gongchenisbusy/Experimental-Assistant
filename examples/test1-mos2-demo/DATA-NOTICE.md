# Demo data notice

The files under `source-inputs/data/` and the screenshots under `site/assets/qa/` are included solely as public demonstration material for Experimental Assistant.

## Included material

- one Raman text export;
- one PL text export;
- one user-annotated AFM PNG with a `0.79 nm` instrument annotation;
- fifteen conversation screenshots showing the corresponding EA workflow.

The source-data hashes are listed in `source-inputs/data/checksums.sha256`. The public Demo does not include credentials, private attachments, browser state, institution sessions, or user-machine absolute paths.

## Interpretation boundary

These materials demonstrate a reviewable workflow. They do not independently prove material identity, layer count, calibration validity, growth mechanism, or whole-sample uniformity. The integrated conclusion applies only to the measured flake and measurement region, subject to the limitations recorded in the public report.
